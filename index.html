The Sacred Circles

The Four Laavan

During Anand Karaj, the couple walks four sacred circles around the Guru Granth Sahib Ji — each round deepening their union with each other and with the Divine.

੧ Pehli Laav

The first round marks the beginning of the divine journey. Led by the Guru's grace, the couple sets forth on the path of righteousness — their worldly and spiritual duties intertwined in love.

੨ Dooji Laav

In the second round, the True Guru is met within the heart. Fear of God fills the soul, ego dissolves, and in its place blooms a profound reverence — the couple draws ever closer to the Divine.

੩ Teejee Laav

The third round symbolizes detachment from the world's fleeting pleasures. The heart overflows with intense longing for God, and the soul discovers its true home nestled in the embrace of the Eternal.

੪ Chauthi Laav

In the fourth and final round, the soul merges with the Almighty — Anand, blissful union, is attained. The couple's hearts are forever woven together, blessed by God's infinite love and light.
